Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a grassroots gathering of developers and tech enthusiasts. Over the years, it has grown into a global event that celebrates innovation, collaboration, and the future of technology.

Our Mission

TechCon is committed to fostering a diverse and inclusive tech community. Our mission is to empower creators, challenge norms, and inspire the next generation of innovators through meaningful dialogue and hands-on learning.

Notable Past Speakers

TechCon has hosted some of the most influential voices in technology:

  • Ada Lovelace – Celebrated for her pioneering work in computing theory.
  • Alan Turing – Honored for his foundational contributions to computer science and cryptography.
  • Grace Hopper – Recognized for her work on programming languages and advocacy for women in tech.
